welcome to twuft.ly

1. search twitter for any public user, no sign-up required

2. see how popular their tweets are with their followers. dynamic data visualization for retweets and favorites. click the data to see each tweet and details.

3. twitter doesn't track followers over time, but twuft.ly does. the first time a user is searched it goes in our database where we stalkerishly watch its followers count.

oh and its free
